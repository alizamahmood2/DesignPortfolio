Portable Stove

Pair Studio Project - Islamabad, 2015

This is a portable stove that people can take hiking, camping etc. It's very compact and comes with additional modules for different types of cooking. The design is complimentary to the natural textures and landscape of our chosen site.

The design is inspired by the stone terraces of the site (in Islamabad) that we chose as a potential use case scenario. This concept of terraces is also used to achieve compactness in the design so that it’s easy to carry and visually not too heavy.

It has a simple pivot mechanism and basic modular structure so that it’s cost effective. It uses modularity to maximize portability and provide options according to user preference.

Made in collaboration with Abdullah Javaid.
