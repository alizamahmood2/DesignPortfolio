Hi! I am a designer, maker and creative based in New York City. I’m currently a 2nd year MFA Design and Technology student at Parsons School of Design. My work ranges from industrial design to interactive installations to more experimental value-driven projects. I’m currently working on my

thesis project


My thesis project seeks to uncover, recover and reimagine materialities of technological objects.

Using creative research practices to deconstruct and study the materiality of existing technological objects, I will create technological artefacts to recover the value that is being lost with each new advancement, and regain the richness of tactile and embodied causality. That in turn aims to foster a greater sense of care, intimacy and responsibility towards the materials we use and interact with.
