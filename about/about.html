Hi! I'm a designer, maker and creative based in New York City. I recently graduated from Parsons School of Design with an MFA in Design and Technology, in May 2020. My work ranges from industrial designs to interactive installations to critical design projects.


My practice is driven by creative research, personal curiosities and value-led inquiries. There are a lot of different materials that I work with, including digital ones, but my favorite making space is the wood shop.


I got my Bachelors in Industrial Design from National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST) from Pakistan, and I spent a year of that degree studying abroad at Middle East Technical University (METU) in Turkey.


These are some fields of practice and domains that I’m particularly interested in: tactile technology, industrial design, play, social impact design, interactive design, speculative design, critical design, material culture and participatory design.


I’ve grown up in multiple cities of Pakistan. I’m bilingual and always excited to learn new languages. Other than the realms of design and art, I love alternative rock music, chai, and astrophysics.
