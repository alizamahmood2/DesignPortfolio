Materialities of Technological Cuboids

Ongoing Thesis Project - New York

Parsons School of Design

This is my ongoing thesis project that seeks to uncover, recover and reimagine materialities of technological objects. It aims to partially reconstruct or reimagine these objects and the space that they exist within.

How can we alter the aesthetic of experience of these objects, to allow for tactile and embodied causality, that can in turn foster a greater sense of care and responsibility towards the materials that we use and interact with?

I believe that uncovering the materiality of technological objects, by drawing back the curtain of the interface, screens and opaque cold materials, has the potential to create spaces that imply tactile causality and a networked responsibility. My work responds to current discussions within design and technology, digital humanities, media archaeology, and tactile technology.
