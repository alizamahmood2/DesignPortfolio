Van for Childbirth


Group Studio Project - Islamabad, 2017

'Hifazat Se'


roughly translates to ‘with care/protection’

Approximately, 1 woman dies every 20 minutes in Pakistan due to childbirth complications.

This service is designed for rural areas of Balochistan where the existing maternal mortality rate is alarmingly high due to home births going wrong because of poverty, illiteracy, lack of proper facilities nearby and lack of transportation to these facilities. These areas have a traditional system of midwives but they are neither trained nor educated.


Hifazat Se aims to promptly provide safe care to both the mother and baby at time of labor/childbirth.
