Dark Labels


Group Project - New York, 2018

Course: Machine Learning

The goal of this project was to highlight the negative stereotypes and effects that can come with being labeled. We used NYC Crime Data to show how people could potentially be seen through the eyes of the victim or suspect.

We used a dataset from the NY Open Data Initiative that shows the percentages of crimes committed in NYC by race.

It includes:

o Victims

o Suspects

o Arrestees

o Population

o Races of NYPD officers

Made in collaboration with Jason Agyekum and Serena Liu.
