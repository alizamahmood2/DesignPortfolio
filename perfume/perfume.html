Perfume Bottle


Individual Project - Islamabad, 2016

It was the middle of spring and I (an asthmatic person) serendipitously happened upon a place in Islamabad that was densely populated with eucalyptus trees. The first breath of fresh air that I inhaled, felt as though it cleared up my enitre respitory system after a month of feeling like my windpipe was constricted and thin - this moment of relief and joy is a personal one and the experience that I wanted to bottle.


I aimed to have the familiarity of holding an inhaler to reflect in the bottle but to simultaneously break away from the medicinal aspect of it and present something elegant and luxurious.



Ideation

The spray mechanism is the combination of an aerosol spray and asthma inhaler pump technology.
