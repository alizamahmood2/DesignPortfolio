Washbasin Units


Group Studio Project - Ankara, 2016

Won in Students Undergrad Category at National Furniture

Design Competition, Turkey 2017. Got Awarded at Design

Week Turkey in Istanbul, 2017.

This is a family of products that aims to increase hygiene habits for children by providing smart, fun and eco-friendly solutions. The main product is a washbasin unit to be installed in groups in primary schools. The first-hand research was based in Ankara, aimed at primary schools. It is a self-cleaning unit that is meant to be operated by the janitor after school breaks. The lid can be closed and locked with silicon sealing. Within 10 seconds, the 3-minute cleaning cycle starts, that uses minimal water and cleaning liquid due to maximum pressurized coverage provided by the piping in the lid. Parts can be disassembled and replaced. The goal is to decrease the overall water and energy consumption of the bathroom by using smarter design of washbasins, implementing water and energy saving strategies, and encouraging the user to use the bathroom more responsibly.

In a team of 4, we worked on the majority of this project together. Every team member worked equally and with active communication. I produced the product renders and technical drawings displayed here. The clay model was made by our team mate who had previous experience with clay.



Made in collaboration with Ibrahim Elshamy, Serhat Ünlü and Başak Laçin.
