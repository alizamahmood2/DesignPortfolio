Cardboard Furnitue

Pair Studio Project - Islamabad, 2015

This was an intense 2 day project in which we had to construct a cardboard model of a 9 foot tall piece of furniture from the late Baroque Period: 'Secretary (sécretaire à abattant), ca. 1815–20 with later additions'.

At the time, we could only find 2 photographs of it online and the main dimensions (105 x 37 x 21 in.).

The model turned out to be slightly thinner and crooked since we had to cut pieces by hand and assemble them in half a day!

^These were the images we used to figure out the proportions and base our model on.

We made a quick 3D model to figure out the sizes and number of sheets of cardboard we would need.

^These were some sketches for figuring out the different parts.

^Left: This was a quick lego model, partially to help clarify proportions and partially for fun. Center & Right: This is a scaled down model.
